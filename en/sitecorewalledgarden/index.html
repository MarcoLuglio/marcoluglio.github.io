Gartner Magic Quadrant

In Gartner Magic Quadrant, Adobe Experience Manager seems to be the current leader, but it is written in Java (no surprise there, given the choice for previous products such as ColdFusion). Others being Acquia (PHP), Episerver (C#) Liferay (Java) and Sitecore (C#) naturally. Again, the world seems to be divided between the two usual suspects, with an alternative for developers that frown upon more formal languages.

Now, if you are wondering why not just call it the CMS magic quadrant and make everyones lifes easier, it used to be called just that. And for what developers may care the most, that is probably where we will spend most of our time. But, as you may already have suspected, developers are not the focus of these platforms. In fact, progamming languages are not even mentioned in the homepage os the aforementioned products, and you will only find about them after some digging.
